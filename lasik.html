Lasik Services.

Why LASIK

LASIK eye surgery is the best known and most commonly performed laser refractive surgery to correct vision problems. Laser-assisted in situ keratomileusis (LASIK) can be an alternative to glasses or contact lenses.In LASIK surgery, precise and controlled removal of corneal tissue by a special laser reshapes the cornea changing its focusing power.

How long does LASIK last for?

While the results of LASIK surgery are permanent, the advantages may diminish over time. The results of LASIK will last a lifetime for the majority of patients.

Will you need glasses after LASIK?

In short, in the vast majority of cases, you will not require distance glasses after LASIK surgery. You will only require reading glasses at the same (or nearly the same) age that you would have required them if you had not undergone LASIK surgery.

Can i drive home after LASIK?

You must not drive on the day of the surgery. As a result, you'll need to arrange for someone to drive you home. You may feel fine right away, but your eyes may be sore and your vision may be blurry, making it unsafe for you to drive.
